AI Researcher / Project Lead May 2026 - Present

Neuromorphic Ear-to-Chest ECG Reconstruction with Perceptual Loss

Developed a quantized Spiking Neural Network autoencoder that reconstructs clinical-style chest ECG from noisy ear-worn ECG using 4-bit LSQ Conv1D layers, MultiSpike activations, fixed-point input quantization, and a composite medical time-series objective.

  • Combined MSE, ECGFounder perceptual feature loss, Total Variation smoothing, and Pearson correlation loss.
  • Built subject-level train, validation, and test splits across 36,407 two-second segments from 12 paired recordings.
  • Reached 0.8508 Pearson correlation on an unseen patient and 0.9065 after decoder-only personalization.
  • Profiled 13,684 parameters and 13.44M MACs per 2-second window for ultra-low-power wearable AI.
Open repository
Project Leader Oct. 2025 - Dec. 2025

Edge AI Product Recognition via 16-Stream RTSP

Built a high-throughput real-time detection pipeline on NVIDIA Jetson Nano using DeepStream, GStreamer, Docker, TensorRT, and YOLOv8n.

  • Processed 16 concurrent RTSP streams on constrained edge hardware.
  • Evaluated FP16 and INT8 quantization, knowledge distillation, and architecture tuning.
  • Benchmarked robust detection at target distances up to 10 meters.
